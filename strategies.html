Our Process

Research first. Risk always.

Our investment process combines in-depth fundamental research with technical analysis to identify opportunities, manage risk, and position portfolios for long-term success. Our disciplined analysis helps identify opportunities with attractive risk-reward characteristics — where the potential upside outweighs the associated risks.

01

Fundamental research

We study the underlying health and value of an investment — the foundation of every decision we make.

02

Technical analysis

We read market trends and timing to refine entries, exits, and positioning across each model.

03

Risk-reward discipline

We act only where the potential upside meaningfully outweighs the risk — and move to safety when it does not.
